# Staffing Model

## Site Security
- Minimum: **2 armed guards per site**.
- Unarmed guards may extend coverage only when armed minimum is met.

## Executive Protection (EP / PPO)
- Minimum: **1 PPO per protected person per shift**.
- Multiple PPOs may be assigned to one protected person.
- Non-primary PPOs constitute Rapid Response Team (RRT) supporting primaries.

## Shifts
- Standard coverage model: **3 x 10-hour shifts/day**.
- Guard goal: **4 days/week, 40 hours/week**.
- Training day cadence: schedule to maintain 40-hour target and prevent OT.
